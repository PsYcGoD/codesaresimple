# Codes Are Simple

[https://codesaresimple.marketingstudios.in](https://codesaresimple.marketingstudios.in)

Codes Are Simple is a self-learning technology platform for beginners who want to move from zero confidence to practical digital skills.

It is built for learners who need patient teaching, clear steps, and real practice instead of confusing jargon. Each lesson is designed to explain the idea first, then let the learner practice, answer checkpoints, complete tasks, and finish with a capstone project.

## What Learners Can Study

- Python from beginner to practical builder
- WebDevelopment with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, design, and deployment
- APIs and automation through ConnectOps Lab
- Data, databases, and AI analysis through InsightAI Lab
- Machine learning and AI agents through AgentMind Lab
- Cyber basics and ethical hacking foundations through CyberShield

## How It Works

- Self-paced learning
- Step-by-step sessions
- Short teaching levels
- Checkpoints after important concepts
- Practical tasks during the session
- Capstone projects at the end
- Progress tracking and unlocks
- Tier completion certificates
- Scholarship support for learners who need help

## Who It Is For

- School students
- First-time coders
- Parents looking for structured learning
- College students who feel behind
- Working adults changing direction
- Small business owners learning technology
- Anyone who wants to learn without feeling intimidated
